# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026

### Added
- Room booking, inquiry, and reservation management
- Real-time room availability dashboard
- Guest check-in and check-out system
- Room service and laundry billing
- Restaurant and bar management with KOT support
- Table management and multiple price menus
- Cashier-wise cash management
- Inventory and purchase order tracking
- Inter-location stock transfers
- Comprehensive MIS reports (room, guest, financial, operations)
- User authentication and role management
- Advance booking with deposit collection
- Bill transfer between restaurant and room accounts
- Daily collection flash reports
